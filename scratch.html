Gardening

How To Get Started Beginner Tips Planting Guide Garden Game

Growing up, my family loved planing our garden every year and enjoying the fruits of our labor, including homeade salsa! I highly recommend gardening to anyone who's interested in trying. Here's how to get started:

  1. Prepare a spot
    • Choose a spot in your yard with soft dirt or use a large container/planter box and fill it with dirt
    • Clear the dirt of any rocks
    • Loosen the dirt with a shovel or tiller
    • Pour nutrient-rich soil on top and lighly mix it in with some of the dirt, keeping most of the soil on top
  2. Choose your plants
    • This is the fun part! You get to choose from countless plants which ones you want to grow in your garden!
    • Depending on how much space you have, I'd recommend selecting 3 - 8 different types of plants and get a few of each kind
    • You can buy the seeds directly, but I like buying seedlings (small plants) and then just transplanting them into my garden
  3. Care for your garden
    • Whether using a sprinkler system, drip irrigation, or hand-watering, make sure your plants are being watered regularly
    • Make sure your plants are getting enough sunlight
    • Keep an eye out for weeds and remove them as needed
  4. Harvest your crops
    • When your produce is ready and ripe, collect the fruits or vegetables and enjoy the fruit of you labors!
    • Get creative with different ways you can prepare and cook with your fresh produce
    • Share your harvest with friends and family!
